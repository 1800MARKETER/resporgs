About Resporgs.com

Resporgs.com is one site in a network of 12, all built by one person, all aimed at making every toll-free and local vanity number in North America visible. This page is where that bigger picture lives.

The network Resporgs.com lives in

Hover any finger‑hole to visit that site. Resporgs.com is the only public RespOrg directory in the network — the reference data layer beneath every number lookup.

The center plate goes to VanityNumbers.com. The 7‑o’clock hole is the one you’re standing on.

The person behind it all

Bill Quimby

The original 1-800 MARKETER. Online since 1995.

Bill Quimby has been in the vanity number business since 1995, when he registered TollFreeNumbers.com. He's been the leading independent source and voice for toll free and local vanity numbers for over three decades. He hasn't just gotten a lot of vanity numbers for customers, he's helped them get the numbers themselves, and helped the whole industry. The site has been online and evolving the whole time, from a keypad of links in the 1990s to a whole network of big data sites powering the best lookup tool in the industry today.

Resporgs.com is the public directory layer beneath that work — the only place anywhere that names every Responsible Organization in the toll-free system, shows what they hold, and traces how numbers move between them.
